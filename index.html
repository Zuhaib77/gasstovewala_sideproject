How It Works

Our Repair Process

A transparent, thorough 4-step process, so you know exactly what we do with your appliance.

1

Inspection

We carefully assess your stove or HOB to identify gas leaks, blockages, burner faults, or any performance issues before any work begins.

2

Safe Dismantling

We carefully dismantle burners, nozzles & relevant parts following safety protocols to ensure no damage to your appliance during the repair process.

3

Deep Steam Service

Advanced steam servicing of burners, nozzles and internal parts to restore full airflow and flame efficiency. Your stove performs like new.

4

Final Testing & Cleanup

Full functionality check of every burner, ignition and valve, followed by post-service cleanup. Handed back clean and working, with your 15-day warranty.
